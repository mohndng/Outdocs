About This Project

This tool is designed to assist Filipino students in their thesis writing journey by leveraging the power of Google's Gemini AI. It helps generate thesis titles and rationales based on user-provided keywords, streamlining the initial phase of academic research.

Our goal is to provide a simple, intuitive, and powerful tool to overcome writer's block and accelerate the thesis formulation process.
